Build a customer support chatbot that answers product questions, checks order status, and escalates sensitive issues to a human agent.

Business context:
- The chatbot will be used by ecommerce customers in the US.
- It should answer product FAQs, retrieve order status, and create a support ticket when escalation is needed.
- It must avoid exposing sensitive customer information.
- It should provide clear logs for troubleshooting and quality review.
